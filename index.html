PR or ER

A cinematic take on gym culture, where ambition gets loud and limits get blurry

PR or ER

It’s not a highlight reel. It’s the in-between. The quiet days, the routines that look the same but feel different. This project captures the rhythm of showing up — not for the drama, but for the lift that might be better than the last. No fails. No PR glory shots. Just focus, tension, and a camera that romanticizes repetition. It’s less about the moment you hit it and more about the work that almost does.

Shot handheld with natural and directional gym lighting. Graded neutral with low contrast. Edited in Premiere with rhythmic cuts to emphasize form and pacing.

Shot on DJI Osmo Pocket 3
